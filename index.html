La-Mulana Remake item graph

This graph shows you how all items are related to each other, based on the Randomizer's requirements
You can zoom by holding shift and scrolling the mouse wheel
You can click on various nodes, which makes it show all the nodes required to get to the selected one

This does have a few issues though due to there being cyclic requirements. Also, not every edge connecting to a node might be required, the game often has multiple ways to get a single item, and I've not found a way yet to properly group specific edges.
